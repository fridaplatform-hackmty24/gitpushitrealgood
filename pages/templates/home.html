Getting Started With GreenGuard.

GreenGuard revolutionizes greenhouse management with advanced monitoring technology. Our system provides real-time insights into plant health and early detection of potential diseases.

Intelligent.

Advanced deep learning model optimized with Intel's OpenVINO™ toolkit. Provides real-time, critical analysis of plant vitality, ensuring you receive precise and actionable insights on your greenhouse vegetation condition.

User-Friendly.

Our intuitive dashboard presents complex data in an easy-to-understand format. Get a quick overview of your greenhouse's health or dive deep into detailed analytics with just a few clicks. Customizable alerts keep you informed without overwhelming you.

Customizable.

Tailor GreenGuard to your unique greenhouse: Customize for various crop types, growth stages, and facility sizes. From detection sensitivity to reporting preferences, our advanced app adapts to your specific needs, ensuring precise disease detection and plant health monitoring.

Secure.

Your data's security is our priority. GreenGuard uses state-of-the-art encryption and follows best practices in data protection. Access controls ensure that your sensitive greenhouse data remains private and secure at all times.

How GreenGuard Works.

Create Your Greenhouse

Create a digital twin of your greenhouse in the app: Input your facility's layout, dimensions, and crop arrangement.

Submit Greenhouse Pictures

Bring your plants into focus: Simply snap and share images of your crops with GreenGuard.

Data Analysis

GreenGuard's AI analyzes your crop photos, detecting subtle signs of stress or disease and providing practical recommendations for your greenhouse's health.

Optimize

Apply GreenGuard's customized recommendations to optimize your greenhouse management. Adjust care routines, environmental controls, and treatments based on data-driven insights to enhance crop health and production.

Key Features of GreenGuard.

GreenGuard offers a comprehensive suite of features designed to revolutionize greenhouse management. From advanced monitoring to predictive analytics, we provide the tools you need for optimal plant health and productivity.

Cloud-Based Monitoring

Access your greenhouse data anytime, anywhere. Our cloud-based system ensures real-time updates and secure storage of your valuable information.

Predictive Analytics

GreenGuard's machine learning models are trained on thousands of both healthy and infected plant images, guaranteeing highly accurate health assessments.

Bacterial Threat Detection

Our AI algorithms detect potential bacterial threats. Receive early warnings and targeted treatment recommendations to protect your crops and maintain optimal plant health.
